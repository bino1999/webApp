Introduction

Welcome to the Embrace Empower Mental Well-Being Project. This project aims to address the global issue of mental health, particularly focusing on depression, through the use of advanced AI technology. Our mission is to provide accessible mental health support and promote overall well-being.

Research Project Scope

Our research aims to explore how an AI-based chatbot can predict depression and provide personalized mental health activities. We target university students, employees, and clinical patients to offer support and resources tailored to their unique needs.

Research Objectives

The primary objective of our research is to develop a comprehensive web application called "Embrace" that tackles loneliness and provides a safe space for individuals to connect, share, and seek support.

Predict depression using an AI-based chatbot.

Offer personalized mental health activities.

Create a positive and respectful chat platform.

Analyze sentiment from diary entries to offer advice.

Methodology

We employed a multi-step methodology including data collection, model development, integration, and deployment. The process involved:

Collecting and preparing datasets for depression and mental health activities.

Training and optimizing machine learning models using neural networks.

Integrating the models into a dynamic chatbot within a web application.

Testing and validating the system with user feedback for continuous improvement.

Technologies We Used

We utilized a variety of technologies to develop the Embrace application:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for understanding and predicting user emotions.
  • Machine Learning for personalized activity recommendations.
  • Web Development frameworks to create an interactive and user-friendly interface.
